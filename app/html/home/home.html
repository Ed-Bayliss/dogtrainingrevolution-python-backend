What Makes Our Life Skills Program Special?

Our puppy training classes focus on the 10 Life Skills every puppy needs, including:

  • Confidence-building: Helping puppies feel safe and secure in their environment.
  • Surprise tolerance: Teaching puppies to handle sudden changes with calmness.
  • Handling and self-control: Preparing puppies for vet visits and grooming with ease.
  • Learning to listen and follow rules: Ensuring your puppy knows how to behave in every situation.
  • Making the right choices: Encouraging puppies to think and act in ways that reduce tension and promote harmony.
These skills are taught in practical, real-world environments to ensure your puppy is well-prepared for daily life.
