Why Choose Our Puppy Training Classes?

Small Group Sizes for Personalised Attention

We limit our puppy classes to small, select groups to provide each owner and puppy with the intensive, one-to-one tuition they deserve. Our professional trainers give you the personal focus that larger classes simply can’t offer, ensuring both you and your puppy receive the highest level of support and guidance.

Real-Life Training for Real-World Results

Our Life Skills for Puppies curriculum emphasises training in real-life situations. From learning how to be calm and confident in public to managing everyday challenges like walking on a lead or meeting new people, your puppy will develop essential life skills that integrate seamlessly into your day-to-day life.

Lifetime Support and Premium Resources

When you enrol in our puppy classes, you gain access to our exclusive members’ area, filled with high-quality resources to support your puppy's ongoing development. From video tutorials to expert advice, you will benefit from a lifetime of professional guidance, ensuring that both you and your puppy are set up for long-term success.

What Makes Our Life Skills Program Special?

Our puppy training classes focus on the 10 Life Skills every puppy needs, including:

  • Confidence-building: Helping puppies feel safe and secure in their environment.
  • Surprise tolerance: Teaching puppies to handle sudden changes with calmness.
  • Handling and self-control: Preparing puppies for vet visits and grooming with ease.
  • Learning to listen and follow rules: Ensuring your puppy knows how to behave in every situation.
  • Making the right choices: Encouraging puppies to think and act in ways that reduce tension and promote harmony.
These skills are taught in practical, real-world environments to ensure your puppy is well-prepared for daily life.
Science-Based, Modern Training Techniques
At Dog Training Revolution, we use only science-based, positive reinforcement methods that are rooted in the latest research. Our trainers continually expand their knowledge through ongoing professional development, ensuring your puppy benefits from the most up-to-date and effective training techniques available.
