Welcome to Dog Training Revolution

Where Excellence in Puppy Training Begins

At Dog Training Revolution, we offer exclusive, results-driven puppy training based on the acclaimed Life Skills for Puppies program developed by Lincoln University. Our approach goes beyond basic obedience, focusing on life skills that empower your puppy to grow into a confident, well-mannered dog ready to face any situation.

Get in Touch Rachel Trafford is an ABTC and IAABC-certified dog training professional, bringing decades of experience and a proven track record of success to every class. Known for her pioneering approach, Rachel was honoured with the People Pet Award for Innovation by The Mirror in 2024. As the resident dog training expert for Moorlands Radio, Rachel offers practical advice to struggling dog owners during live phone-ins.

Rachel has served as an advisor to numerous rescue charities and frequently consults on complex behavioural referrals. With her qualifications in counselling, she brings a unique blend of empathy and expertise to support clients through the adjustments needed to address canine behavioural challenges. Rachel also collaborates closely with veterinary surgeons, ensuring that clients receive comprehensive, high-quality guidance for even the most complex behavioural issues.

Get in Touch Laura has an in-depth practical background working with large guarding breed dogs, gaining her knowledge and extensive skills with such dogs from working all over the country. She has spent years studying canine psychology and genetic influences and the impact such things can have on companion and working dogs to apply this in her behavioural work.

Laura has also hosted popular training workshops for professionals across the UK, collaborating with other industry experts. She often mentors other dog trainers and behaviourists in the hopes of improving and supercharging the effectiveness and quality of dog training that is offered to owners in the future.
