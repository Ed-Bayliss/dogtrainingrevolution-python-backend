Puppy Classes

Junior Class

Our Junior Classes are perfect for adolescent dogs aged 4-12 months who have completed basic training or are ready to continue their learning journey. These sessions focus on reinforcing foundational skills, improving impulse control, and addressing common teenage behaviors such as jumping, pulling on the leash, and recall issues.
